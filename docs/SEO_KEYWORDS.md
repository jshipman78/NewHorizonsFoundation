# New Horizons Foundation — SEO Keyword Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the SEO keyword strategy for New Horizons Foundation to maximize visibility with grant funders, donors, partners, and those seeking mental health services and programs.

## Keyword Placement Strategy

### Homepage

**Title Tag:**
```
New Horizons Foundation | Mental Health Nonprofit | Veterans, Youth & Community Programs
```
(~60 characters)

**Meta Description:**
```
New Horizons Foundation is a national nonprofit advancing mental health access, supporting veterans and first responders, and developing future leaders through ethical, evidence-based programs.
```
(~160 characters)

**H1:**
```
New Horizons Foundation
```

**H2 Examples:**
- Advancing Mental Health Access & Community Resilience
- Our Four Pillars of Service
- Supporting Those Who Serve Our Communities

## Schema Markup Recommendations

### Organization Schema (Homepage)
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "NonprofitOrganization",
  "name": "New Horizons Foundation",
  "alternateName": "NHF",
  "url": "https://www.newhorizonsfoundation.org",
  "logo": "[logo-url]",
  "description": "A national nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing mental health access, education, cultural enrichment, and leadership development.",
  "nonprofitStatus": "501(c)(3)",
  "areaServed": {
    "@type": "Country",
    "name": "United States"
  },
  "knowsAbout": [
    "Mental Health Services",
    "Veteran Mental Health",
    "First Responder Wellness",
    "Youth Leadership Development",
    "Community Mental Health"
  ],
  "sameAs": [
    "[facebook-url]",
    "[linkedin-url]",
    "[instagram-url]"
  ]
}
```

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
```

## Tracking & Measurement

### Key Metrics to Track

1. **Organic Traffic**
   - Total organic sessions
   - Organic traffic by page
   - Traffic by keyword

2. **Keyword Rankings**
   - Position tracking for Tier 1-2 keywords
   - Featured snippet opportunities
   - Local pack appearances

3. **Engagement**
   - Bounce rate by organic landing page
   - Time on page
   - Pages per session

4. **Conversions**
   - Donation form completions
   - Contact form submissions
   - Newsletter signups
   - Partner inquiry form submissions

### Tools to Use
- Google Search Console (required, free)
- Google Analytics 4 (required, free)
- Ahrefs or SEMrush (recommended, paid)
- Microsoft Clarity (user behavior, free)
